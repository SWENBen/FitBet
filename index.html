Frequently Asked Questions

  • What fitness watches do you support?

    Both FitBit watches and Apple watches can connect to our app. We plan on adding further support though!

  • How many people can I invite to a fitness challenge?

    We currently support up to 20 people in a challenge.

  • What kind of things can we bet on?

    There's a wide range! You can bet money between each other with a minimum entry bet, bet on a verbal agreement and even create fitness tournaments.

  • Is there a cost to use the service?

    The only cost is the small amount of money it takes to process the money transfer.
